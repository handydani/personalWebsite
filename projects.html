Ron Paul Smith

Also known as 'Project RPS', this COP3503 final project was the result of us wanting to make something worth putting on a resume. I lead a team of 6 programmers in writing a computer vision project by using OpenCV libraries to take a picture of your hand, make a binary image, and match that image against a small database we collected ourselves. We used this functionality to make a rock, paper, scissors game so that the user can play with the computer.
