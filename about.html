About

I wish I could say concretely what I'm interested in but that would be dishonest. I'm interested in a lot of things, including but not limited to cyber security, UX/UI, web development, and graphic design. Ultimately, I love being able to spot problems and fix them with software. I've written code on everything from matching pictures to a database, to picking a random episode of Futurama. However, the little programs, like the latter, are more for my own enjoyment than for any real use.

Professionally, I love big picture ideas, leadership, solving problems, mentoring someone, and feeling like an integral part of a team.

Personally, I love Spanish food, making a playlist every semester, sushi, organizing events, field days and barbecues, podcasts, traveling, baking for friends, a Song of Ice and Fire, salsa, bachata, art museums, cinema, Fire Emblem, being able to teach people the things I know, theater, and dogs.
